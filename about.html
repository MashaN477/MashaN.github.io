Before Science, There Was Magic

As a kid, I always marveled at how the world functioned, even though we can't see the mechanism as to why it works. I became fascinated by all aspects of life - how our eyes squint up at the sunlight, how birds stomp their feet on the ground to simulate rain that tricks the earthworms into coming out, how some pine-cones only open up with the presence of fire. Every step has its purpose.

I was determined to learn about the many mysteries of the universe, and the more I learned, the deeper my craving for knowledge became. I created 'Before Science There Was Magic', with the goal of sharing the profound nature behind the most common as well as unique experiences that exist today.

Utilizing the scientific method is just as important to me as valuing intuition. In this blog I will also write about the triumphs and tribulations the scientific community has faced as human thought and behavior continues to change over time.

Huge thank you for stopping by - I hope that after reading these stories, your world feels a bit more magical :).

Why Now?

When misinformation runs rampant, you deserve to know the Truth. The rise of modern technology and the internet has made it easier than ever to learn, but it has also made it easier for us to believe something is true, when it is dangerously false. My goal is to shed light on sources you can trust, without sacrificing your time or energy.
