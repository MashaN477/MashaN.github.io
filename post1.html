My First Blog Post

Published on November 17, 2025

Welcome to the first post!

This is going to be an extremely informal intro, where I tell you all about why I started this digital archive.

The Braindump

Here are several reasons why I decided to create this space:

  • Quick-Facts I can't put into words how fun science is, but how time-consuming getting through scientific papers can be. I want to share the interesting stuff I learn without making it feel like a chore.
  • I Love You - Here, Read This Paper Sharing knowledge is a prime mode of connection for me, and if there was a fifth love language, I would name it "sending you articles that I thought would be relevant and cool".
  • CodingThis is a major coding project for me. Expect some articles on AI, machine learning, and quantum mechanics.
  • CreativityLab reports have their place, but developing a unique writing style in a way where I can express my ideas clearly is also a skill I value heavily.

What to Expect

In future posts, I'll be sharing stories about current topics in science, how things have changed, and parallels I notice between different aspects of my life. Some posts will be more personal, while others might be more practical and informative.

Thank you for being here at the beginning of this journey. I hope you can't tell what part of this was ai and what part was my own doing :)/p>
